AYUDA SOLUTION WELCOME
The AYUDA PLATFORM is a solution that has been created by the Affine Group and presented here as a proof of concept only, as this solution is likely to be subject to review by regulators in multiple jurisdictions. It is a modular, e-commerce platform that uses a matching engine to deliver five core products and services designed to address the COVID19 pandemic.
  • Respirators and other Personal Protective Equipment
  • Instant Test Kits
  • Contact Tracing App with user privacy
  • Knowledge and Education sharing starting at the community level, moving to global
  • Digital 'Health Care Professionals' or Avatars and Holograms supervised and trained by practitioners
